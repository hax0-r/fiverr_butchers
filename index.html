About Us

Known for our warm welcome and homely atmosphere, The Butchers is a true community hub where locals and visitors alike come together to relax, eat well, and catch up. We take great pride in our menu, offering a wide selection of dishes made with the finest locally sourced ingredients, each one carefully chosen to ensure quality and flavour.

Whether you’re here for a hearty meal, a pint with friends, or to catch the latest match on Sky Sports, you’ll find that The Butchers of Llanddarog delivers every time. Located in the charming village of Llanddarog, we’re more than just a pub, we’re a place where memories are made and friendships flourish. So come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warmth and flavour that make The Butchers so special.

Dining With Us

At The Butchers of Llanddarog, dining is more than just a meal—it’s a warm, homely experience rooted in quality and local flavour. Our menu is built around the best locally sourced ingredients, with dishes that celebrate Welsh tradition and countryside comfort. From pub favourites to daily specials, every plate is made with care, offering something for every appetite.

Whether you're joining us for a leisurely lunch, a family dinner, or a special occasion, you’ll enjoy a relaxed setting, friendly service, and food that speaks for itself. We’re always happy to accommodate dietary needs and pride ourselves on creating a welcoming space for all. Come hungry, leave happy.

Relaxed Ambience

From the moment you walk through the door, you’ll feel the calm, welcoming atmosphere that makes The Butchers of Llanddarog so special. Our pub is a cosy retreat where rustic charm meets friendly hospitality—think wooden beams, a crackling fire in the colder months, and the comforting hum of good conversation all year round.

Whether you're catching up with friends over a pint, enjoying a quiet meal for two, or settling in to watch the game, our laid-back setting makes it easy to relax and feel at home. It’s the kind of place where time slows down, and every visit feels like a well-earned break from the everyday.
